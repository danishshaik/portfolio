IMAZE

A web application/game that allows users to upload images, that are then used to generate mazes. A user can choose to either take on the maze by himself or challenge a friend and race in real time. Imaze takes advatage of the Aviary API, the Firebase API, and the 500px API. Featured on TechCrunch.

WAFFLE

A location based feed of photos, in the form of a simplistic mobile web application. Waffle creats a radius of 0.5 miles around you, and every relevant photo is shown on a real time newsfeed. Ever image you upload will be visible to all your friends, around your area for a 24 hour period.
